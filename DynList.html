DynList

Manage your lists in a simple and dynamic way on your Android Device

Description

DynList is an Android App that allows to manage your lists divided by category and in multiple tabs:

  • by adding and removing user-defined categories;
  • by adding and removing tabs with customized titles;
  • by adding, removing or temporary hiding items within every tab.
on your Android device.
