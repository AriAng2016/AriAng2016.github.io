DynMemo

Manage your notes in a simple and dynamic way on your Android devices

Description

DynMemo is an Android App that allows to manage dynamically your own lists and notes by:

  • defining user-defined categories;
  • creating, within evey category, multiple tabs with customized titles to insert your own lists;
  • adding your items in your lists;
  • swiping items you want temporary hide in a list;
  • moving items between tabs;
  • adding reminders to your items.
